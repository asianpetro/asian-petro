About Asian Petro

Fueling a Better and Greener Future

Asian Petro is the flagship consumer brand of Shree Atulya Amrit Private Limited, a fast-growing petroleum and energy solutions company dedicated to redefining the future of fuel, lubricants, and clean energy in India.

Founded with a vision to become a nationwide trusted brand, Asian Petro is more than a fuel company—it is an energy partner that combines innovation, transparency, and reliability to serve industries, businesses, and individuals alike.

Our Commitment

At Asian Petro, we believe energy is the backbone of progress. That’s why our mission is to deliver high-quality, reliable, and cost-effective fuel and lubricant solutions while setting new benchmarks in safety, efficiency, and customer experience. We are committed to building an ecosystem that supports India’s growth by ensuring zero adulteration, complete transparency, and doorstep convenience.

Our Vision

To build Asian Petro into one of India’s most respected national energy brands, known for trust, transparency, and technology-driven solutions, while expanding into biofuels, industrial oils, and eventually establishing our own refinery base.

Why Asian Petro?

Unlike others in the industry, Asian Petro is built on integrity and innovation. While competitors focus on services, we focus on creating value: Nationwide Expansion Plan. Affordable Pricing, Premium Quality – Making world-class lubricants and fuel solutions accessible. Future-Ready Infrastructure – From modern petrol pumps to smart fuel delivery systems. Job Creation & Community Growth – Each outlet and service hub generates employment opportunities, supporting local economies.
